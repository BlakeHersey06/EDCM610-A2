Blake Hersey has 3 Hobbies

My First Hobby

Gaming

I have always loved gaming! For the past three years I have had the honor of being my school's Esports coach. I love the fact that this is a sport that attracts those kids who don't normally fit into other areas. My favorite games to play are flight simulators and Strategy Games.

My Second Hobby

Reading

I absolutely love reading, especially anything related to history and warfare. Aerial or armored warfare especially. I am currently reading two books. Panzer Leader by Heinz Guderian and Fighter Pilot by Robin Olds.

My Third Hobby

Visiting Museums

When my family and I go on trips together I am the one that always tries to drag them along to explore different museums. This includes museum ships like the USS Winsconsin in Norfolk, Virginia or the USS Laffey in Charleston, South Carolina. One day I hope to visit all of the museum ships in the United States.
